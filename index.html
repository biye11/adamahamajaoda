Overview

Debuzy performing at Bostos 2024

Debuzy, born Adama Hamadjoda, is a Cameroonian singer,songwriter,record producer and plays Instrument such as Guitar and Piano. He was born on May 20, 1995, in Mokolo, Far North, Cameroon. Debuzy is one of the leading figures in the Folklore, Afro-Sahel genre and has gained national recognition for his music.Furthermore,he is a reseacher at the University of Yaounde I in the Department of Philosophy. Also a researcher at the Mayo-Tsanaga Cultural Heritage Reasearch Center, the French Transhumanist Technoprog Association(AFT),and the Cameroonian Society of committed Conservators(SCCE).
Likewise,he is the Author of the Book "The value of school in the Mafa traditon","Sounds of the Mountains"-",The promotion of tribality for sustainable development in Cameroon"- "Manaouda Malachie :The star of the Renaissance?", and many others and articles in particular "Towards a theocompatible African Transhumanist?" African Philosophy being his area of specialisation and interested in question linked to decolonization,art and Culture.

Early Life &Education

Haman Adama Hamadjoda is a young research student at the University of Yaoundé I, at the Faculty of Arts, Letters and Human Sciences, in the Department of Philosophy. He works on the question of decolonization of Africa and on the perspective of a theocompatible African transhumanism. He did his primary education at the Primary School of Application III Mokolo Far North Cameroon. He also studied in the same city at the Lycée Classique et Moderne de Mokolo. Since obtaining his Baccalaureate, he enrolled at the University of Yaoundé I. He continued his studies at the same time as making music. He works in collaboration with the Chronomoov Studio label and HH Production. He also produced several scientific works.

Music Career

Debuzy rose to fame in 2019 with his hit single "Nabila"(2019). Since then, he has released several successful albums and singles, including "Femme Africaine" (2019), "Dume Banni" (2020), and "Zivanda" (2021),"Ditsuma"(2021),"Fils desmontagnes"(2022), "African philosophie "(2023).

Awards

Throughout his career, Hamadjoda has won numerous awards, including Folklore Awards, Mafa Cultural Music Awards, and The you Awards. He continues to be recognized for his contribution to the music industry.
